Welcome to Legae La Rona

We provide love, care, and dignity to our elders in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our dedicated staff is here to support the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of each resident. We believe in treating every individual with the utmost respect and compassion.

Legae la rona old age home is a non-profit organization committed to enhancing the quality of life for the elderly in our community.

Mrs J Phasha

Founder & CEO

Mrs Phasha founded Legae la Rona with a vision to support elderly community members who are often overlooked and ignored.

Our Services

  • 24/7 Nursing Care
  • Nutritious Meals
  • Daily Activities & Companionship
  • Medical Support & Transport
  • Spiritual & Emotional Wellness
